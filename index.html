About Our Lab

Our Mission

We are dedicated to advancing knowledge through innovative research and collaboration across disciplines. Our lab focuses on solving complex research problems in emerging computational paradigms, AI/ML for tiny system etc

Founded in 2025, our research group has grown to include over 10+ researchers working on cutting-edge projects with real-world applications.

Our Vision

We envision a future where data-driven approaches transform our understanding of emerging computational systems and lead to breakthroughs in innovetions for sustainable environment and society

Our work bridges the gap between theoretical computer science and practical end-user applications, creating tools and insights that benefit the scientific community and society at large.

Research Domains

NMP / PIM

Exploring Near-Memory and Processing-in-Memory architectures to reduce data movement and enable high-performance, energy-efficient computing.

3D NoC

Designing 3D Network-on-Chip architectures to enhance scalability, performance, and communication efficiency in modern multi-core systems.

Cloud / Edge Computing

Leveraging cloud and edge platforms for scalable, distributed, and real-time data processing and applications.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Building intelligent and connected systems that integrate sensing, communication, and analytics for real-world applications.

Quantum Computing

Developing algorithms and architectures that harness quantum mechanics for solving complex computational challenges.

Security

Ensuring data privacy, system integrity, and resilience through advanced security protocols and architectures.

Lab Director

Dr. Manojit Ghose

Lab Director & Principal Investigator

Dr. Ghose hail from Bilasipara town in the state of Assam, India. He joined IIITG in July 2019 after completing his Ph.D. (in CSE) from IIT Guwahati. He earned his B.E. in CSE from Jorhat Engineering College (Govt. of Assam) under Dibrugarh University, followed his M.Tech. (in CSE) from IIT Guwahati. His area of research experties are in - NMP, Edge/Cloud, IMP/IoT, Program Analysis, Quantum computing, AI/ML for system, Security etc.

Publications

Deep learning approaches for genomic variant calling from sequencing data

Johnson, S., Chen, M., & Rodriguez, E. (2023). Nature Methods, 20(3), 345-357.

This paper presents a novel deep learning framework that improves the accuracy of variant calling from next-generation sequencing data by 27% compared to existing methods.

PDF BibTeX
Network-based integration of multi-omics data for cancer subtype identification

Chen, M., Anderson, L., & Wilson, J. (2022). Cell Systems, 15(2), 125-139.

We developed a network-based approach that integrates genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic data to identify novel cancer subtypes with distinct clinical outcomes.

PDF BibTeX
Transformer architectures for protein function prediction

Rodriguez, E., Kim, D., & Johnson, S. (2022). Bioinformatics, 38(5), 1321-1328.

This work adapts transformer architectures originally developed for natural language processing to predict protein function from sequence alone, achieving state-of-the-art performance.
